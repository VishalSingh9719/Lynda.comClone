Course description

Do you have an entrepreneurial spirit, but also like the stability of working for an established company? This audio-only course, adapted from the Modern Mentor podcast, aims to help you combine your love of innovation with the comfort of full-time employment. Podcast host Rachel Cooke explains how intrapreneurship enables you to harness your creativity at work while positioning yourself as an invaluable asset. Rachel first defines intrapreneurship and explains who might consider it, and then provides actionable steps to bring intrapreneurship to life in your own organization. She even walks through example scenarios that will help you learn how to pioneer ideas and collaborate with corporate management. This course was created by Macmillan Publishers.
